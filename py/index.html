Everyone loves tuning into their favorite songs on radio and listening to the billboard hits. We intend to provide the same experience by providing hassle free online live radio streaming with options to mute the advertisements or switch the channels based on personalized recommendation during advertisements. Everyone loves things personalized as per their preferences. The core system has a digital feed of multiple online radio stations. It continuously churns the audio data, to recognize songs, chatter (radio chat shows) and ads. This information is then sent to the user’s mobile app/webapp and the radio will channels will switch automatically to another radio channel which has a song being played based on the user’s genre preference. Also, the user can choose to automatically skip ads and chatter.
